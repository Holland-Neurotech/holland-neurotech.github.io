Our Philosophy

A personal journey that led to creating innovative solutions for women's health.

When I was studying Clinical Neuroscience, it occurred to me that many neurological disorders are incurable but can have their likelihood significantly reduced with appropriate preventative health practices. This is maximised for women's health, where current health systems already bias towards men in our understanding of symptoms, diagnostics, and treatments.
As many in health have experienced, this sent me down a rabbit hole of learning and experimenting with different tech. Frustrated by the lack of systems that met my needs or the needs of women like me, I realised that the tools we use must evolve.
