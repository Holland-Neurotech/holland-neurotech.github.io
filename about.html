Our Story

About Holland Neurotech

A Canadian-based company dedicated to developing cutting-edge health technology solutions that empower women to take control of their health through data-driven insights.

Make Tracking Meaningful

The vision behind our mission to revolutionize women's health technology.

The Insight

Holland Neurotech was born from a profound insight in Clinical Neuroscience: while many neurological disorders lack cures, their likelihood can be significantly reduced through targeted preventative health practices. This realization sparked our journey into exploring how technology could help women take control of their health outcomes.

Like many innovations in healthcare, this led our team to explore various health tracking technologies, searching for solutions that could make a meaningful difference in women's lives.

The Challenge

Our research uncovered a consistent problem: existing systems fell short of addressing women's specific health needs. Each platform had strengths but also significant gaps. The technology wasn't designed with women's unique physiology in mind.

Conversations with potential users confirmed this wasn't an isolated observation. Many women were struggling to make sense of data from their wearables and medical appointments, lacking tools that could transform this information into actionable insights tailored to female biology.

The Solution

Holland Neurotech began developing customized systems to help women answer their most pressing health questions:

  • How can I optimize my cardiovascular health and improve my VO2 max?
  • Is my consistently low blood pressure something to be concerned about?
  • How do specific foods affect my insulin levels and overall metabolic health?
  • What preventative measures can I take to reduce my risk of cognitive decline?
  • When am I at my cognitive peak, and how can I optimize my productivity?

This work evolved into Go Go GAIA—a comprehensive platform designed specifically for women's health needs.

GAIA continues to evolve with new tracking capabilities, passive sensors, and algorithms based on the latest research. Our goal is to provide women with cutting-edge tools that deliver meaningful, actionable insights about their health.

Have a health question GAIA doesn't address yet? Let us know—we're committed to expanding our platform to meet the diverse needs of women everywhere.
